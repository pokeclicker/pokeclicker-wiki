Oak Items are special items the player can equip to gain different effects. At first, only one slot is available, but the player can unlock more as they capture more unique Pokémon:

| Slot # | Requirement |
|---|---|
| 1 | 20 Unique Pokémon |
| 2 | 50 Unique Pokémon |
| 3 | 100 Unique Pokémon |
